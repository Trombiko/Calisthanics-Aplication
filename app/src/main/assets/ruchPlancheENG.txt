1. From the starting position, tilt the shoulder ring forward.
2. We immobilize the wrists!
3. Lean forward.
4. When our wrists are at the height of our belt, we tighten our backs tightly. We work as hard as possible with our lumbar section. We raise our legs so that our body is straight!
5. Keep holding. Until our body is in a straight (horizontal) position.
6. We keep figure planche until our legs begin to fall, or until our elbows begin to sag due to lack of strength!
